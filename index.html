About Me

Hello! My name is Devin Belle and this is my portfolio. I am an aspiring full stack developer currently undertaking a coding bootcamp in the hopes of breaking out into the tech industry after graduating. My work experience includes a combination of customer service and warehouse management working at companies such as XPO Logistics, Amazon, and Cox Transportation services. During the 2020 pandemic, my interest in coding started with small self-made projects before I started taking this field as a serious endeavor. In this portfolio are the University of Richmond BCS bootcamp projects I have worked on in the past 6 months. Each project demonstrate my abilities working with coding languages such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more.
